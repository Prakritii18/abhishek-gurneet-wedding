Need help?

FAQ & Contacts

Answers to common questions, travel & stay details, and who to call if you need anything on the day.

Is there transport from the hotel to each venue?

Yes — a shuttle runs from the hotel lobby 45 minutes before every event. Timings are also posted at the concierge desk.

Are children welcome at all events?

Absolutely — all events on both days are family-friendly.

What should I wear on Day 2, since there are multiple venues?

Traditional attire (with a head covering) for the Anand Karaj at the Gurudwara, and formal/cocktail for the evening Reception. Full dress code details are below.

Where do I upload my photos from the day?

Head to the Pictures tab and use the shared Google Drive link — anyone can upload or download.
